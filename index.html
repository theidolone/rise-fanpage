Who is Rise?

Rise Kujikawa is a fictional character from Persona 4, which originally released in 2008, later remastered as Persona 4 Golden in 2012 by ATLUS.

Originally having her concept art based on Lisa Silverman from an earlier Persona title, she takes her name and finalised appearance from her voice actor, Rie Kugimiya.

Quick Trivia

  • Rise and Lisa (Rise's concept inspiration) both share the Lovers arcana.
  • Rise is the sixth member of the Investigation Team, not joining in on combat, but instead acting as the Navigator for the team.
  • Rise's Persona is Himiko, which can later evolve to Kanzeon by maxing out her Social Link.
  • Kanzeon can yet again evolve into Kouzeon by talking to Rise after 1/10 while her Social Link is maxed out.
  • In Persona 4 (2008), Kanzeon had lower stats than Himiko despite being an evolution. This was changed in Persona 4 Golden (2012) so that Kanzeon instead mimicked Himiko's stats.
  • Kouzeon learns a skill unique from Kanzeon despite sharing stats, "Complete Analysis", which reveals all enemies' elemental affinities.
